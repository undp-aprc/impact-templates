UNDP Climate Change Adaptation (CCA)

UNDP is one of the leading UN Agencies assisting countries on adaptation to climate change. We provide support to implement on-the-ground interventions to reduce vulnerabilities to climate extremes; we support enabling policy, institutional, social, economic, environmental and political conditions to better manage the impacts of climate change as they unfold in developing countries, especially least developed countries.

In 2016, our work has progressed from ideas to projects, from action to results. This is reflected in our signature programmes.

UNDP Climate Change Adaptation

Gender Impacts

Foood and nutritional Security

A significant implication of climate change and the resulting decline in agricultural production and food security in many parts of the world is that women are playing an increasingly important role in provisioning food for the family.

Strategies for increasing food production and provisioning

  • Niger

    Over 25,000 farming families in seven regions have had access to improved varieties of millet and sorghum (grown by men) and cowpea (women's domain) which, combined with more intensive use of chemical inputs, has led significant increase in yields and revenues.
